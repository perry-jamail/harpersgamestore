Welcome to Harper's Game Store!

We are a Denton-based collectable-card-game (CCG) and hobby store with a primary focus on anything and everything Magic: The Gathering! We welcome one and all to come check out our in-person store located just off the Denton Square! We also run MTG tournaments weekly, spanning all major formats! Come check us out!

Mission

Our mission at Harper's Game Store is to provide everyone with the highest quality products, customer service, and tournament experience possible while also expanding and developing this community that we love!

Products

  • Sealed MTG Products
  • CCG Accessories
  • Various Board Games
  • Sealed Snacks/Drinks

Who Are We?

Perry and Mackenzie Jamail (along with their adorable Shiba-Inu/German Shepherd mix, Harper!) make up the owners and primary staff at Harper's Game Store!

Both Perry and Mackenzie have multiple years of experience playing and enjoying all different kinds of MTG formats in casual and competitive play.
